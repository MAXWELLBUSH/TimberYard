Our Products

Hardwood Trees

Durable and high-quality hardwood for all purposes.Denser, more durable, and slower to grow. Hardwoods are typically more expensive. Ideal for fine furniture, flooring, cabinetry, and high-quality wood products.

Softwood Trees

Lightweight and versatile softwood for construction.Commonly used in construction, furniture making, paper, and packaging materials.

Enginered Wood Sources

Man-made from layers or fibers of wood glued together with adhesives.Offers strength, stability, and uniformity, often used in construction. It can be designed to be more sustainable and cost-effective.

About Us

Established in 1920, Miti Timberyard has been providing top-quality timber products to customers for over 50 years. Our mission is to deliver reliable, sustainable timber solutions for all your needs.

We take pride in offering a wide range of timber products and exceptional customer service.

Our Core Values

  • Quality: Providing only the finest timber products.
  • Sustainability: Promoting eco-friendly practices and responsible sourcing.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Ensuring a seamless experience from order to delivery.

Our Facility

Located in the heart of Eastleigh, Nairobi, our state-of-the-art facility is equipped to handle a variety of timber processing and storage needs. We take pride in maintaining a clean, organized, and efficient yard.
